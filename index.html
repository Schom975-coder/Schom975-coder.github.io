About Scott Hom

I'm a DevOps Engineer II at Disney Streaming, based in Jersey City, NJ, where I build and maintain the infrastructure that keeps streaming services online for millions of viewers around the clock. My work spans Kubernetes deployment pipelines, parallel CI/CD systems, Docker containerisation, and cloud infrastructure automation.

Before Disney, I spent three years at BGC Partners engineering the deployment infrastructure for the ForexMatch financial trading platform - a zero-downtime environment with exacting reliability standards. Earlier in my career I interned at Deloitte Consulting and Maryville Consulting (Accenture), gaining early exposure to enterprise software delivery.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from Washington University in St. Louis (Magna Cum Laude, 3.84 GPA) and am currently completing an M.Sc. in Technology Leadership at Brown University (Class of 2026). I'm also a certified AWS Solutions Architect and Kubernetes Administrator (CKA).
